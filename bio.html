Bio

01/2019 - 11/2023 | Postdoctoral research

Topic : I started a postdoc with Andrea Brovelli at Institut de Neurosciences de la Timone. I studied how complex cognitive functions are encoded by distributed networks. The goal was to identify local encoding, pairwise and higher-order interactions supporting the implementation of reinforcement and causal learning in humans using intracranial EEG.

Key publications : Combrisson et al., NeuroImage (2022); Combrisson et al., JOSS (2022)
